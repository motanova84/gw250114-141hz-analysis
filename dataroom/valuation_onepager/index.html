    QCAL Valuation One-Pager¶

    CONFIDENTIAL - For Investment Discussion Purposes

    Executive Summary¶

    QCAL (Quantum Coherence Analysis for LLMs) is an open-source framework that applies quantum gravity principles to gravitational wave analysis and LLM coherence evaluation. The project has achieved 100% detection rate across LIGO/Virgo detectors and demonstrates >95% hallucination reduction in LLM systems.

    The Moat¶

    1. Reproducibility as Competitive Advantage¶

    • DOI-referenced validation: All methods published with DOI 10.5281/zenodo.17445017
    • Lean 4 formal verification: Mathematical proofs machine-verified (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.17379721)
    • Open science standard: Reproducible pipelines set industry benchmark
    • Academic credibility: Peer-reviewable methods attract research partnerships

    Result: Competitors cannot replicate our scientific rigor without years of equivalent validation work.

    2. Public IP as Barrier¶

    • Published DOIs create prior art, preventing patent trolling
    • MIT License enables widespread adoption while maintaining attribution
    • First-mover advantage in quantum coherence metrics for LLMs
    • Network effects from open-source community contributions

    Result: Open source creates a moat through standardization and community lock-in.

    3. Dual-Market Positioning¶

    • Scientific research: Gravitational wave analysis (LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA)
    • Enterprise AI: LLM hallucination reduction (>95% improvement)
    • Cross-pollination: Quantum gravity methods applied to AI coherence

    Result: Diversified revenue streams with synergistic technology transfer.
